Privacy Policy

This page explains what information LykoonAdds stores and why.

Information we store: your username, email address, phone number, wallet balance, task completion history, and the withdrawal account details (Easypaisa/JazzCash number and name) you submit for payout.

Why we store it: to run your account, credit task rewards accurately, send your email verification code, prevent duplicate/bot accounts, and send your withdrawal to the right place.

Who can see it: only the LykoonAdds admin team, for the purpose of approving campaigns and processing withdrawals. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Security: passwords are stored using Django's built-in salted-hash system (never in plain text). Sessions use HttpOnly, SameSite cookies.

Your choices: contact the site admin to request a copy of your data or to have your account and associated data deleted, subject to any pending withdrawal records we're required to keep for accounting purposes.
